# I-HealthConnect — CHW Quick-Reference Guide
## Urubuga rwa CHW — Inyigisho Ngufi

---

## 1. What is I-HealthConnect? / I-HealthConnect ni iki?

**English:**
I-HealthConnect is a smartphone app that helps you identify pregnant women who may have a baby with a health problem before birth. It uses the health information you already collect to calculate a risk score — no ultrasound needed.

**Kinyarwanda:**
I-HealthConnect ni porogaramu ya telefone igufasha kumenya ababyeyi batwite bashobora gutwita umwana ufite ikibazo cy'ubuzima mbere yo kuvuka. Ikoresha amakuru y'ubuzima usanzwe utangaho kugira ngo ibarure amanota y'inzitizi — nta echographie irakenewe.

---

## 2. When to use it / Ugikoresheje ryari

**English:** Use I-HealthConnect for every pregnant woman you visit:
- At any antenatal care (ANC) visit
- When a woman reports reduced fetal movement
- When you notice high blood pressure or low hemoglobin
- When a woman has a family history of birth defects

**Kinyarwanda:** Koresha I-HealthConnect ku mubyeyi wese utwite usura:
- Ku musuriro wose w'ubuvuzi bwa mbere y'accouchement (ANC)
- Iyo umugore abwira ko umwana we agenda gake
- Iyo ubona umuvuduko w'amaraso uri hejuru cyangwa hemoglobine iri hasi
- Iyo umugore afite umuryango wagize ikibazo cy'imvuka

---

## 3. How to screen a patient / Gukora isuzuma ry'umurwayi

**English:**
1. Open the I-HealthConnect app on your phone.
2. Tap **"New patient screening"** on the home screen.
3. **Step 1 — Enter patient profile:** name, age, gravida, parity, gestational age.
4. **Step 2 — Enter measurements:** blood pressure, fundal height, glucose, hemoglobin, weight.
5. **Step 3 — Answer risk history questions:** family history, prior pregnancy loss, current infection, folic acid use.
6. Tap **"Run anomaly prediction"** and wait up to 30 seconds.
7. Read the result and follow the recommended action.
8. Tap **"Send referral"** if the result is red or amber.

**Kinyarwanda:**
1. Fungura porogaramu ya I-HealthConnect kuri telefone yawe.
2. Kanda **"Gusuzuma umurwayi mushya"** ku ipaji nyamukuru.
3. **Intambwe ya 1 — Injiza amakuru y'umubyeyi:** izina, imyaka, inshuro yo gutwita, inshuro yo kubyara, ibyumweru by'inda.
4. **Intambwe ya 2 — Injiza ibipimo:** umuvuduko w'amaraso, uburebure bw'inda, isukari, hemoglobine, uburemere.
5. **Intambwe ya 3 — Subiza ibibazo by'inzitizi:** umuryango, gutwita bishize nabi, indwara ubu, acide folique.
6. Kanda **"Shakisha inzitizi"** uragume akanya gashobora gufika kuri amasegonda 30.
7. Soma ibisubizo ukurikire inama itangwa.
8. Kanda **"Ohereza intumwa"** niba ibisubizo ari umutuku cyangwa umworoherane.

---

## 4. Understanding the result / Gusobanukirwa ibisubizo

| Color / Ibara | English meaning | Insobanuro mu Kinyarwanda | English action | Icyo gukora |
|--------------|----------------|--------------------------|----------------|-------------|
| 🟢 **GREEN** | Low risk | Inzitizi nke | Continue routine antenatal care. Next visit in 4 weeks. | Komeza kubungabunga bisanzwe. Garuka mu by'iminsi 28. |
| 🟡 **AMBER** | Elevated risk | Inzitizi izo hejuru | Schedule a follow-up visit within 2 weeks. Monitor blood pressure. | Tegura gusurwa mu by'iminsi 14. Kurikirana umuvuduko w'amaraso. |
| 🔴 **RED** | High risk | Inzitizi nyinshi | Refer immediately to district hospital for ultrasound. Do not delay. | Ohereza vuba kwa muganga w'akarere ku echographie. Ntutinde. |

---

## 5. What to do for HIGH RISK / Icyo gukora iyo inzitizi ari nyinshi

**English:**
**Do this immediately when you see a RED result:**
1. Tell the mother calmly that she needs a hospital check-up.
2. Tap **"Send referral"** in the app to notify the district hospital.
3. Write a referral slip and give it to the mother.
4. Call the district health center to inform them she is coming.
5. Record the visit in your paper register as normal.
6. Follow up within 48 hours to confirm she attended the hospital.

**Kinyarwanda:**
**Kora ibi vuba iyo ubonye ibisubizo bya UMUTUKU:**
1. Bwira umubyeyi neza ko akeneye isuzumwa kwa muganga.
2. Kanda **"Ohereza intumwa"** muri porogaramu kugira ngo ubwire indaro y'akarere.
3. Andika urupapuro rw'intumwa uruhe umubyeyi.
4. Hamagara ikigo nderabuzima cy'akarere ubwire ko azaza.
5. Andika urwibutso mu gitabo cyawe bisanzwe.
6. Garuka mu masaa 48 kugira ngo ubone ko yagiye kwa muganga.

---

## 6. Data and privacy / Amakuru n'ibanga

**English:**
All patient information is saved only on your phone and is encrypted (protected). Data is only sent to the health system when you have internet or mobile data, and only anonymized statistics are shared — never patient names. Your data belongs to the Ministry of Health Rwanda.

**Kinyarwanda:**
Amakuru yose y'umurwayi abikwa gusa kuri telefone yawe kandi afunzwe (arindwa). Amakuru yorohererwa sisitemu y'ubuzima gusa iyo ufite interineti cyangwa data, kandi amakuru ya rusange gusa nta mazina y'abarwayi. Amakuru yawe ni ay'Ubuzima bwa leta y'u Rwanda.

## 7. Technical problems / Ibibazo by'ikoranabuhanga

| Problem / Ikibazo | Solution (English) | Igisubizo (Kinyarwanda) |
|-------------------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **App won't open** / Porogaramu ntiyafunguka | Restart your phone and try again. If it still fails, uninstall and reinstall the app. | Fungura telefone kabone uyisubire. Niba bikomeje gucika, siba porogaramu uyisubireho. |
| **"Run prediction" button is greyed out** / Buto ntishobora gukoreshwa | Make sure all required fields are filled in. Look for fields with a red border. | Menya neza ko imirima yose yuzuye. Reba imirima ifite umupaka w'umutuku. |
| **Result won't sync / upload** / Ibisubizo ntibisindagirwa | The app works offline. Results will upload automatically when your phone connects to Wi-Fi or mobile data. | Porogaramu ikora nta interineti. Ibisubizo bizorohererwa vuba iyo telefone yawe ifashe Wi-Fi cyangwa data. |
